Features

The Church Management Systems have vital features which you can use to organise your church data in a very organised manner and also help you present accurate reports.

Secretary platform

Used by church secretery. Register, edit and delete church members information( Church Membership Forms). Assign(AVED) members positions. Add(AVED) Church commitees. Add(Upload) church minutes. Check Church Stacs.Birthday Notifier. Add(AVED) year plan.

Treasure platform

Used by treasure. View monetary value of Assets, Tithes , Income and Expenditures. Register(AVED) Church assets. Add(AVED) Income(Tithes, Freewill. etc). Add(AVED) expenditures. Add(AVED) Year Budget Plan. Check Money Usage and balances. View Statements.

Detailed Reports

On Treasures"s platform. Provides printable reports. Yearly Recap Reports on dashboard (graph - income by expenditure). Yearly Balances, Monthly balances, daily balances, Income balances, Expenses balance and printable Yearly budgets.
